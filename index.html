I’m a traditional naturopath. My husband is a minister. For years, we served our communities—healing bodies and nurturing souls. But deep down, we dreamed of something more: a life of adventure, purpose, and freedom.

So we took the leap.

We walked away from our stable careers, packed our bags, and moved to Mexico—not to retire, but to reinvent our lives. And here’s the crazy part: We now fund our entire new journey (plus his charity work with indigenous churches across Central & South America) through our affiliate marketing side hustle!

No more 9-to-5. No more "someday." Just passive income that grows while we explore, learn Spanish, and wake up to ocean views. Every sale we make helps support the causes we’re passionate about—and fuels our next adventure.

Our mission? To prove that you don’t need to wait for "the right time" to live the life you want. Whether you dream of traveling, making an impact, or finally escaping the grind, we’re living proof it’s possible—and we’re here to show you how.

If two "regular" people like us can do it… why not you?

Our Journey to Itzocal: A Hidden Gem in Mexico's Mountains

October 2021

Our Journey to Itzocal: A Hidden Gem in Mexico's Mountains

High in the rugged mountains of La Huasteca, Northern Hidalgo, lies Itzocal—a village so remote that our first visit in October 2021 was the first by outsiders in 28 years. This isn't just a place on the map; it's a community that has become part of our story, our purpose.

The road to Itzocal is a test of determination, winding through dense forests and over rocky terrain, often rendered impassable by fierce storms. But every challenge is worth it when we arrive and are greeted by the warm smiles of Miguel and his family, the heart of the local mission.

Miguel and his kin are Nahuatl, indigenous people whose resilience and spirit are as strong as the mountains around them. For years, they've been the backbone of their community, providing spiritual guidance and support in a place where resources are scarce. It's hard to believe that just 10 hours from the Texas/Mexico border, there are people who must venture into the mountains to gather wood just to cook their meals on outdoor stoves. Yet, this is the reality of life in Itzocal.

Our connection with this village began with a simple desire to help, but it has grown into something much deeper. Since 2021, we've supported the mission monthly with monetary gifts and personal visits, bringing not just supplies but also love, encouragement, and a reminder that they are not alone. Each trip is a chance to connect, to share stories, and to make a tangible difference.

One memory that stands out is the joy on Miguel's mother's face when she received a pair of reading glasses. For the first time, she could see the words on the page clearly—a small gift that opened up a world of possibilities. Moments like these remind us why we do what we do.

In Itzocal, we've found more than just a place to visit; we've found a community to support and love. It's a testament to the power of human connection and the impact we can make when we step out of our comfort zones. This village, with its breathtaking views and humble people, has shown us that adventure isn't just about seeing new places—it's about touching lives and being touched in return.

If two "regular" people like us can turn our dreams into reality, so can you. Whether it's traveling to remote villages or supporting causes close to your heart, every step you take can change lives—including your own.
